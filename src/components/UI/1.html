Sri Lanka, a picturesque island in the Indian Ocean south of India, is the oldest source of sapphires in the World. This country, also known as Ratna-Dweepa (Island of precious stones), is the source of 200 minerals that have been classified as precious stones. Their beauty, durability and rarity combined, make these sophisticated gems in high demand throughout the world. Writings of several traders, dating all the way back to the fourth century attest that they crossed the Indian Ocean to obtain these gems. Even Marco Polo wrote that Sri Lanka Is the source of the most beautiful sapphires in the world.

All gemstones in Sri Lanka were formed during pre Cambrian geological age.Therefore, any gem you select is over 800 million years old.

Gems have been first recorded in the historical tradition of the island around 500BC. Therefore, Sri Lanka's gem mining industry is one of the oldest of its kind in the world. Sri Lanka gems cover the full range of the colours of the spectrum. Hence you could find a gemstone to suit any mood,any time of the day or season of the year. The Blue Sapphire is Sri Lanka's Gem Supreme. Sri Lanka's Blue Sapphires are the finest in the world. Sapphires of the finest quality have what is called by the experts a corn flower blue or royal blue tint.
